    Reel Eats is an innovative snack ordering app designed to enhance the movie-going experience by allowing patrons to order their favorite snacks and beverages directly from their seats. The app aims to streamline the ordering process, reduce wait times, and provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for movie theater customers.

    Background

    The cinema industry has been continuously evolving to enhance the overall movie-going experience. With the rise of streaming services, traditional movie theaters are focusing on offering unique and superior in-theater experiences to draw audiences. Concession sales, a significant revenue stream for theaters, have often been hampered by long lines and wait times, leading to customer dissatisfaction.

    Problem Statement

    Patrons often face long lines at concession stands, especially during peak times.

    The traditional method of ordering snacks at the counter is time-consuming and inefficient.

    Theatre staff can be overwhelmed during busy periods, trying to manage long queues.

    Goal

    Our snack ordering app for movie theaters will let users order snacks easily and effortlessly which will affect movie goers by allowing them to filter through snacks that fit their budget and dietary needs. We will measure effectiveness by analyzing the number of daily and weekly snacks ordered

    Target Audience

    • Frequent Moviegoers
    • Casual Moviegoers
    • Casual Moviegoers
    • Couples
    • Young Adults and Millennials

    Approach

    The development and implementation of Reel Eats follow a structured and user-centric approach to revolutionize the snack ordering experience in movie theatres.

    User Research

    Research Goals

    • User behavior: To understand the typical behavior patterns of users when it comes to ordering snacks at the movie theater, identifying the frequency of snack orders, preferred timing (before, during, or after the movie) etc
    • User needs and expectations: To determine factors that influence their decision-making, like convenience, variety, pricing and speed of service
    • User experience: identify pain points and areas where the current process can be improved to ensure user satisfaction and streamline the overall experience

    Interview Questions

    1. Can you describe your typical experience when ordering snacks at a movie theater? FU: How often do you order snacks, and at what point during the moviegoing experience do you usually do so?
    2. What factors influence your decision to order snacks at a movie theater? FU: What do you consider when choosing which snacks to order?
    3. How important is the speed and efficiency of the snack ordering process to you when moviegoing?

    Research Findings

  • Time efficiency: Users expressed a desire for a quick and efficient snack ordering process.
  • Convenience and flexibility: Users emphasized the importance of convenience and flexibility in the app
  • Clarity and information: Users highlighted the need for clear pricing information.
  • Accessibility: Users with accessibility needs expressed the importance of screen reader compatibility and adjustable font sizes.
    • Some potential features I could consider building into my app to address them are:
    • Pre-ordering and advanced scheduling options
    • Saved order history and quick reordering functionality
    • Delivery and pickup options for added convenience
    • Customization options for snack orders
    • Integration with seat selection or reservation system.

    By incorporating these features into my app, I can address the concerns and problems that are important to my users, providing them with a convenient, efficient, and inclusive snack ordering experience.

    Strengths

    Cinemarks strengths:

    • Strong presence
    • Exclusive rewards
    • Integrated experience

    GoPuffs strengths:

    • Convenience and speed
    • Extensive product range
    • Wide operating hours

    AMCs strengths:

    • Enhanced movie experience
    • AMC stubs program
    • Wide movie selection

    Weakness

    Cinemarks Weaknesses:

    • Limited availability
    • Potential pricing concerns
    • Less emphasis on snack ordering

    GoPuffs weaknesses:

    • Limited to delivery service
    • Restricted product selection
    • Delivery fee and minimum order requirements

    AMCs weaknesses:

    • Limited availability
    • Complex loyalty program

    Gaps

    • Both movie apps are specific theater chains, leaving out users who prefer other theater brands
    • Competitors don't offer extensive customization options for snack orders
    • None of the competitors have seamless integrations with popular food delivery platforms or restaurant partnerships
    • Competitors have room for improvement in terms of providing real-time updates on order status and estimated wait times

    Opportunities

    • Collaboration with multiple theater chains
    • Offer extensive customization options
    • Explore partnerships or integrations with popular food delivery servicesÂ 
    • Real-time updates and proactive communication
    • Multilingual support
